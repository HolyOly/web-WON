EXCHANGES AND RETURNS

If you are not satisfied with an item, you have 60 days after receiving your order to exchange it for any size or request a refund. It’s very simple!

If you are registered, go to Returns in the My Account section. Next, select the 'Home collection’ option and complete your request online. If you are not registered, go to the My purchases section and enter your e-mail address and order number. In the order detail, click on 'Request exchange or refund' and select the ‘Home collection’ option to complete your request. Next, you will receive a confirmation e-mail with full details of your request and the day of collection.

If you prefer another size, you can request it at the time of return. To do this, indicate the item you wish to modify and the new size on the delivery note. If the size in question is not available, we will refund the amount of the garment. If you wish to replace the garment with another model or colour, you will have to return the item and place a new order. Personalised garments and accessories cannot be exchanged or returned.

When making exchanges and returns, please bear in mind that:

  • Any items returned must be in the same condition in which you received them.
  • Face masks, protective screens and hand sanitizers cannot be exchanged or returned for health and safety reasons.
  • Customised garments and accessories cannot be exchanged or returned.
  • For reasons of hygiene, panties, thongs and culottes are fitted with a safety seal to prevent them being tried on or worn. If you wish to return this type of item, the label and the safety seal must be conserved.
  • For reasons of hygiene, earrings cannot be exchanged or returned.
  • When we receive your package, we will inspect the condition of the items before proceeding to exchange the size or reimburse the returned items. Bear in mind that in the case of postal returns, Mango will not accept carriage due or payment on delivery and will not pay the delivery or return charges.
  • Items purchased as a set must be returned together. Individual items cannot be returned separately.
  • Items that vary in price according to size (towels, sheets, duvet covers, etc.) cannot be exchanged, only returned.
